# FieldBrief

## Mission

FieldBrief exists to help law enforcement officers quickly understand trustworthy legal and operational changes before going on duty.

FieldBrief does not replace legal authority.

FieldBrief makes official legal authority easier to understand.

---

# Vision

Every officer should be able to begin a shift confident that they know:

- What changed
- Why it matters
- What applies to them
- Where the information came from

All in less than five minutes.

---

# Product Philosophy

We are not building another legal database.

We are building an operational intelligence platform.

Every feature should answer one question:

> "Does this help an officer make a better operational decision?"

If not...

It probably doesn't belong.

---

# Principles

## 1. Official Sources First

Every legal update should always reference the original authority.

Examples:

- U.S. Supreme Court
- State Supreme Courts
- State Legislatures
- Attorney General Opinions
- POST Standards
- Department Policy

AI is never the authority.

AI explains the authority.

---

## 2. Plain English

Everything should be understandable by an officer reading it before briefing.

Avoid unnecessary legal language.

Explain operational impact.

---

## 3. Transparency

Every update should answer:

What changed?

Why does it matter?

Officer takeaway

Official source

Confidence

---

## 4. Speed

The home screen should answer one question:

"Am I operationally ready for today's shift?"

Everything else belongs somewhere else.
